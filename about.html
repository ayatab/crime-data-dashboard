This smart dashboard displays Seattle crime locations on a map as well as presents visualizations of statistics concerning that same data. The goal of this project is to provide easily accessible and up to date maps and statistics for purposes related to travel and potential living situations. It could also be informative to law enforcers as to what areas may need stricter patrolling.

Disclaimer: The crime data used in this project is only of finalized crime reports, not necessarily all crimes that have occurred. It is also important to note that any entry missing a latitude or longitude coordinate was filtered out of the data.

The data used for this project comes from the City of Seattle Open Data found here.
